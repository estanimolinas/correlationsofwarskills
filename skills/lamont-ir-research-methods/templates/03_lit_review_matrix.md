# Template 03 — Literature review matrix

**Lamont chapter (topic):** The literature review
**Use when:** after Template 02 (research question); before drafting your research design
**Output:** a matrix of relevant sources organised by debate, plus a synthesis paragraph and a gap statement

A literature review is not a summary of everything ever written on a topic. It is a structured argument that *your question matters* and *prior work has not adequately answered it*. The matrix forces you to read for position and gap, not just for content.
