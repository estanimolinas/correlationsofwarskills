# Template 01 — Epistemology brief

**Lamont chapter (topic):** Knowledge and research in International Relations
**Use when:** at the very start of a project, before you commit to a method
**Output:** a one-page statement of your epistemological and ontological position and what it implies for your method choice

A research design that does not declare its epistemology tends to leak inconsistent assumptions into every subsequent stage — operationalisations that do not match what the researcher claims to be measuring, evidence that does not match the kind of claim being made. Filling this in early prevents that.
